Discover the Magic of Music Tech!

I am keen to see other musicians use technology to help them get more out of their music. Feel free to download a copy of my free quick guide to creating music using a computer. The guide concentrates mainly on a general outline but also focuses more specifically on MIDI and virtual instruments.

Please visit my blogs to read, learn and provide feedback and comments about your experiences. I would love to hear how you are using technology to help with your music. I have included some videos of myself playing virtual instruments to showcase possible sounds available in software.
