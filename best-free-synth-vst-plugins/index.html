Why synth plugins matter

A synth is often the most flexible plugin in a beginner setup because it can play so many roles in one arrangement.

Even if you prefer acoustic or organic music, a synth can still add subtle layers, pads, or supportive bass parts.

How to choose a free synth

Choose a synth that fits your learning style. Some people want depth and endless control. Others want a smaller instrument that makes sound design easier to understand.

If you are new, one great free synth is usually better than five average ones.

When to upgrade

Upgrade only when you know what is missing: maybe more presets, better workflow, a different style of synthesis, or more polished built-in effects.

Free synths are easily strong enough for learning and for many finished tracks.
