Why EQ matters so much

A mix can sound clearer and more professional with better EQ decisions even before you add expensive plugins.

EQ helps instruments stay out of each other’s way, which is one of the biggest keys to cleaner productions.

How to use EQ more effectively

Try cutting before boosting. Often the problem is too much of something rather than too little.

Use broad moves for tone and narrower moves for problem-solving.

Where to use free EQ plugins

Free EQ plugins are useful on almost everything: vocals, drums, bass, guitars, synths, piano, buses, and even master-bus learning.

They are one of the best categories to master early because the skill transfers to every DAW and plugin ecosystem.
